Rishi Bommasani

I am a Senior Research Scholar at the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence (HAI).

Research & Policy Focus

I research the societal and economic impact of AI, especially foundation models, to advance evidence-based AI policy.

To improve AI governance, I have built the interface between AI researchers and policymakers through several major efforts in global AI policy: the EU AI Act and its Code of Practice, the California Report on Frontier AI Policy, and the International Scientific Report on AI.
